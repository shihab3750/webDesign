About Me

My name is Safin Shihab, and I am a junior pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the esteemed Tandon School of Engineering. I have enrolled in this course with the intention of refining and solidifying my understanding of web design. Although I have prior experience in this field, I recognize the importance of establishing a robust foundation, particularly as much of my previous knowledge has been acquired through self-study and online resources such as Reddit and YouTube. By furthering my education in this area, I hope to ensure that I am equipped with the skills necessary to excel in the dynamic and constantly evolving realm of web design.

Life Now

As a 20-year-old college student, I have experienced significant growth and maturation. I have come to understand that embracing one's individuality is a crucial aspect of personal development. Furthermore, I have gained a deeper appreciation for my cultural heritage and the unique experiences that have shaped my identity. I am currently enrolled in a highly prestigious university within our nation, a testament to my resilience and perseverance as a first-generation college student and child of immigrants. Although I am aware that I have much yet to accomplish, I am optimistic that I am on a positive trajectory and am eager to face the challenges that lie ahead.
