Flight System

A final project for CS-UY 3083, Database course. Used Python Flask, HTML, CSS and SQL for implementation. Clients can purchase tickets or ask booking agents to purchase tickets for them from different airlines. At 70% capacity, tickets would increase by 20%. Clients can also order round-trips or single-trips with search filters for different cities, dates, airlines, or airports. Booking Agents can see their revenue monthly to yearly, clients, history, and current/past bookings. Clients can see when their flight is booked, dates, purchase history, past flights. Airlines can see all agents who purchased under their airline, airports, create new flights and planes, set new airports, view in-depth revenue data, and update any relevant data under the airline.
